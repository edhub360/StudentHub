Data Deletion Instructions

EdHub360 — Facebook Login Data Removal

If you have used Facebook Login on EdHub360 and would like to request deletion of your personal data from our systems, please follow the steps below.

What Data We Store

When you sign in with Facebook, we receive and store the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address linked to your Facebook account
  • Your Facebook user ID (used to identify your account)

We do not store your Facebook password or any financial information from Facebook.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to edhub360help@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Facebook Data Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address in the body of the email
  4. Optionally mention your Facebook account name to help us locate your record faster

We will process your request and permanently delete all associated data within 30 days of receiving your email.

What Happens After Deletion

  • Your EdHub360 account will be permanently deactivated
  • All learning data (quizzes, flashcards, notes, progress) will be deleted
  • You will no longer be able to log in using this Facebook account
  • Active subscriptions will be cancelled — no further charges will occur

Contact

For any questions about this process, email us at edhub360help@gmail.com
