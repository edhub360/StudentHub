Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve your learning experience on EdHub360. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture when you register or sign in via Google, Microsoft, or Facebook.
  • Social Login Data: When you use Google, Microsoft, or Facebook to sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from those services. We do not store your social media passwords.
  • Learning Activity: Quiz scores, flashcard progress, course completion status, and time spent on materials.
  • Device & Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, device type, and pages visited to understand how you use the platform.
  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information helps us deliver and enhance EdHub360:

  • Provide Services: Enable you to access courses, quizzes, flashcards, and notes.
  • Authentication: Use your social login identity (Google, Microsoft, Facebook) to securely sign you in without storing passwords.
  • Personalize Learning: Recommend content, track progress, and adapt difficulty levels to your needs.
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs, improve features, and develop new tools.
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with laws and respond to legal requests when required.

3. Third-Party Services

EdHub360 integrates with the following third-party services for authentication:

  • Google Sign-In: Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Sign-In: Governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • Facebook Login: Governed by Meta's Data Policy. We only request your public profile and email address.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help operate our platform (e.g., cloud hosting).
  • When required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated data. To request data deletion, email us at edhub360help@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

6. Your Privacy Rights

  • Access & Correction: Request a copy of your data or ask us to correct inaccuracies.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable non-essential cookies and unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

7. Security & Contact

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including encryption and access controls.
